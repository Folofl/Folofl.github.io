About me

Hello!

My name is Alena and I am a software developer on the TrakCare Medication Management team at InterSystems.


I am originally from Russia, but I have lived in Boston for several years now -- throughout my studies at Tufts University and as I continue to work in the area.

My Interests

Before declaring as a Computer Science major during my undergraduate studies, I was actually planning to major in Biology. However, after spontaneously taking a few Computer Science classes I quickly fell in love with programming and decided to make the switch.


While I do not have a particular favorite sub-field of Computer Science, I enjoy creating programs, websites and applications that are used in research, health care, education and daily life in general.


In addition to programming, I am interested in research and Japanese Language, and I am excited to work on challenges that involve one of or maybe even combine several of those interests.
